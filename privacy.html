Offerly Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Offerly helps users organize and manage their job search by allowing them to save job postings, track applications, and monitor recruiting progress.

Information We Collect

  • Email address used to create an Offerly account.
  • Job application information you choose to save.
  • Job posting information including company, role, location, and job URL.
  • Optional Gmail integration data when you choose to connect your Gmail account.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide Offerly's job tracking features, including saving applications, organizing your job search, and identifying interview invitations, offers, and other recruiting updates.

Chrome Extension

The Offerly Chrome Extension only accesses the currently open job posting when you activate the extension. It extracts relevant job information from that page and saves it to your Offerly account.

The extension does not monitor your browsing activity or collect information from pages unless you choose to save a job.

AI Processing

Offerly may use AI services to extract structured job information from job postings. This processing is performed only to provide the application's features.

Data Sharing

Offerly does not sell personal information. Data is shared only with trusted service providers necessary to operate the application, including authentication, database hosting, and AI processing.

Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect user information from unauthorized access or disclosure.
